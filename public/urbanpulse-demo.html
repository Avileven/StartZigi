UrbanPulse

Smart city insights from real-time noise & air quality data

Venture Overview

Problem

Cities lack real-time, street-level data on noise pollution and air quality. Municipal decisions rely on outdated surveys and static sensors that miss local hotspots.

Solution

UrbanPulse deploys low-cost IoT sensors across neighborhoods and visualizes live environmental data in a city dashboard for planners, businesses, and residents.

Startup Journey on StartZig

Idea Phase

Founder defined the concept: “What if every street could report its noise and pollution in real time?” A landing page was built inside StartZig to validate interest among urban planners.

Business Plan

Market research showed strong demand from smart-city programs and commercial real estate. Revenue model: subscription SaaS for municipalities + analytics API for developers.

MVP Stage

Built a functional prototype dashboard simulating live sensor data for one neighborhood. Early testers could visualize pollution spikes and download reports.

Funding

Pitched to virtual angel investors inside StartZig. Successfully raised $200,000 in simulated seed funding to expand the MVP.
